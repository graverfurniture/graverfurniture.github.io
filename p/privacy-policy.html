Overview

GF Task respects your privacy. This application is ad-free and does not sell, rent, or share any personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what permissions are used, why they are needed, and how your information is handled.

Application Information

App Name: GF Task

Developer Contact: support@graverfurniture.my.id

Android Permissions Used

This app declares and uses the following permission in AndroidManifest.xml:

  • <uses-permission android:name="android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE" android:maxSdkVersion="32" />
Note: This permission is used only for limited access to read external storage on devices running Android 12 (API level 32) or lower. It allows the app to open and process local files (if needed) without collecting or sharing any personal information.

Purpose of Storage Access

The READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permission is required only to enable the app to access user-selected files (e.g., importing or reading local data). This permission is not used to automatically scan, collect, or upload any personal or sensitive content.

  • The app does not browse your file system automatically.
  • The app does not share any file content with third parties.
  • Access is performed only when you explicitly choose a file within the app interface.

Data Collection

GF Task does not collect or store any personal data. The app does not require internet connectivity for its main features and does not send any user data to remote servers.

Advertisements and Analytics

This application is completely ad-free. GF Task does not include advertising SDKs, analytics tools, or third-party trackers. No behavioral or usage data is monitored or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party service providers that collect or process user data. All operations happen locally on your device.

Data Security

Since GF Task does not transmit or store user data, there is no risk of personal data leakage. Any temporary data processed within the app remains only on your device and is deleted automatically when the app is closed or uninstalled.

User Control

  • You can deny or revoke the storage permission at any time from your device settings.
  • The app will still function with limited features when permission is not granted.
  • No private files or folders are accessed without explicit user consent.

Compliance with Google Play Policy

GF Task fully complies with Google Play’s User Data and Permissions policies:

  • Only essential permissions are requested.
  • No personal or sensitive data is collected or shared.
  • The app does not include advertising SDKs or trackers.
  • Permission usage is transparent and explained in this policy.

Children’s Privacy

GF Task does not target children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe data has been collected in error, please contact us to request immediate deletion.
